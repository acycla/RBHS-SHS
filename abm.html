About ABM:

The ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) strand is designed for students interested in pursuing careers in the fields of finance, business, and entrepreneurship. This strand equips students with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the dynamic world of commerce and industry.

ABM students explore subjects such as accounting, economics, business management, and entrepreneurship. They develop a solid foundation in financial literacy, strategic planning, and business ethics.

Career Opportunities

Upon completion of the ABM strand, graduates can pursue various career paths, including:

  • Accountant
  • Business Analyst
  • Entrepreneur
  • Financial Planner
  • Marketing Manager
  • Human Resources Specialist
