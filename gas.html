Strands News

The General Academic Strand (GAS) provides students with a well-rounded education, allowing them to explore various academic disciplines. It is designed for students who want a flexible curriculum that covers a broad range of subjects.

GAS students have the opportunity to delve into humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and mathematics. The strand emphasizes critical thinking, communication skills, and a deeper understanding of diverse academic fields.

Potential Pathways

While GAS is not specialized in a particular field, it provides a solid foundation for students who may choose to pursue various paths, such as:

  • Liberal Arts and Humanities
  • Social Sciences
  • Education
  • Business and Management
  • Communication and Media Studies
  • Public Administration
