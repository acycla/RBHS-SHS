Strands News

The HE (Home Economics) strand is dedicated to fostering practical skills and knowledge related to home management, family, and consumer sciences. Students explore various aspects of nutrition, culinary arts, textiles, and family resources.

HE students gain hands-on experience in cooking, sewing, and other domestic skills. They learn about healthy living, budgeting, and effective family management, preparing them for roles in homemaking and related fields.

The curriculum emphasizes the development of essential life skills, including meal planning, food preparation, and nutrition. Students also engage in projects that enhance their understanding of interior design, textile arts, and resource management within a household context.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the HE strand can pursue diverse career opportunities in home-related and consumer-focused fields, including:

  • Home Economist
  • Culinary Arts Specialist
  • Textile and Apparel Designer
  • Family and Consumer Sciences Educator
  • Event Planner
  • Interior Decorator
