Strands News

ICT:

The ICT (Information and Communication Technology) strand focuses on preparing students for the fast-paced world of technology. Students in this strand explore various aspects of information technology, programming, and digital communication.

ICT students gain practical skills in software development, network administration, and digital media. They learn to solve real-world problems using technology and contribute to the innovation and advancement of the digital landscape.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the ICT strand can pursue exciting career opportunities, including:

  • Software Developer
  • Network Administrator
  • Web Developer
  • Database Administrator
  • IT Consultant
  • Digital Marketing Specialist
