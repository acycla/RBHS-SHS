Strands News

The HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) strand provides a comprehensive exploration of the diverse fields within the realms of humanities, social sciences, and the arts. This strand fosters critical thinking, effective communication, and a deep understanding of societal structures and cultural dynamics.

HUMSS students delve into subjects such as literature, philosophy, sociology, history, and political science. They gain insights into human behavior, cultural diversity, and the historical context that shapes the world we live in.

Emphasizing analytical skills and social awareness, HUMSS graduates are well-prepared for a variety of career paths, including:

  • Journalist
  • Social Worker
  • Public Relations Specialist
  • Historian
  • Human Resources Professional
  • Community Organizer
  • News Anchor
  • Cultural Anthropologist
